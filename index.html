What is QuestCoin:

QUEST coin is a virtual cryptocurrency for DeepWeaver universes, based on peer-to-peer technologies. Initial supply is created solely from quests, puzzles and storyline system. Later we reward gitHub commits, art-illustration and many other jobs focused on improving our worlds. Note: Sometimes roadmap will not progress forward until x amount of quests are solved.

What is DeepWeaver:

Blockchain cities in VR are awesome, but without a story, it looks more like a flat template. Immersive interesting worlds require proper planning and outlined art canvas, total freedom may lead to diversity Overflow, graphical chaos, and questionable usability. Our unique distribution system structures our worlds and our platform puts tools and connections in hands of those who want to create their own virtual worlds.

For more info please look around our site - feel free to contact us. We value our community and want to gather free creators from around the world
